What is Project Uno?

Evox Uno is a unified hub designed to consolidate every Evox application, messaging, transit, productivity, and social, into a single, cohesive platform with a shared account system and real-time sync.

The Vision

Instead of switching between separate apps, Uno provides a single entry point for the entire Evox ecosystem. Your chats from Epsilon, transit data from OASA, school tools from Jeanne D'Arc, and task management from Tasco — all accessible within one interface, with a universal search, unified notifications, and cross-app workflows.

Built on a modular architecture, Uno loads only the services you need, keeping the experience lightweight while offering the full power of every Evox product. Offline support, end-to-end encryption, and a responsive design ensure it works everywhere — from desktop browsers to mobile home-screen apps.

Core Capabilities

Six pillars powering the Uno experience.

💬

Unified Messaging

All your Epsilon conversations, group chats, and channels in one place with real-time delivery, read receipts, and media sharing — no app switching required.

🚍

Live Transit

OASA real-time bus tracking, route planning, and arrival predictions baked directly into Uno with offline timetable caching.

🎓

Campus Tools

Jeanne D'Arc school management, yearbook, and peer connection features — integrated with your unified Evox profile.

📋

Tasks & Notes

Tasco-powered task lists, notes, and reminders synced across all devices. Create tasks from chat messages or transit alerts with one tap and stay organized effortlessly.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Florida notification engine aggregates alerts from every module, applies priority rules, and delivers a single noise-free notification stream.

🔐

Single Sign-On

One Evox account grants access to every service. Secure, token-based authentication with optional biometric unlock on supported devices.
