The truth behind Evox

Evox started in 2019 as a small project focused on creating useful apps for personal and everyday use. Most of these apps were built using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, without any frameworks. While this approach worked well at the time, modern applications require better performance and scalability.
Evox Uno introduces a new way of building apps, focused on speed, user experience, and quality.
With Evox Uno, users can enjoy Evox apps beyond the browser, with full support across all devices.

The Vision

Our vision for Evox is simple: build fast, reliable apps that feel effortless to use.
We believe software should be accessible everywhere, not limited by browsers, devices, or complex setups.

With Evox Uno, we aim to create a platform where apps are lightweight, performant, and designed with the user experience first.
The goal is to continuously evolve Evox into a modern ecosystem of tools that simplify everyday tasks and connect people through intuitive technology.

Core Capabilities

Six pillars powering the Uno experience.

Unified Messaging

All your Epsilon conversations, group chats, and channels in one place with real-time delivery, read receipts, and media sharing — no app switching required.

Live Transit

OASA real-time bus tracking, route planning, and arrival predictions baked directly into Uno with offline timetable caching.

Campus Tools

Jeanne D'Arc school management, yearbook, and peer connection features — integrated with your unified Evox profile.

Tasks & Notes

Tasco-powered task lists, notes, and reminders synced across all devices. Create tasks from chat messages or transit alerts with one tap and stay organized effortlessly.

Smart Notifications

Florida notification engine aggregates alerts from every module, applies priority rules, and delivers a single noise-free notification stream.

Single Sign-On

One Evox account grants access to every service. Secure, token-based authentication with optional biometric unlock on supported devices.
